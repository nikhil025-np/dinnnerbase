🍽️ DinnerEase — AI Weekly Meal Planner

Your tired brain deserves a break.
No planning. No thinking. Just simple dinner suggestions based on what you already have.


🧠 The Problem
After a long workday, deciding what to cook feels impossible. Most people either order food (expensive, unhealthy) or skip meals entirely. The real issue isn't cooking — it's the decision fatigue that comes before it.

💡 The Solution
DinnerEase removes all daily cooking decisions.

Set up once on Sunday in under 5 minutes
AI generates a 7-day personalised meal plan from your pantry
Every evening: open app → see today's meal → start cooking

No browsing. No thinking. No friction.

📱 How It Works
1. SUNDAY SETUP (30 seconds)
   └── Select available pantry items (tap to confirm, no typing)
   └── AI generates your full 7-day meal plan

2. DAILY USE (every evening)
   └── Open app
   └── See: "Today's Meal: Masala Egg Rice (15 mins)"
   └── Tap 'Start Cooking' → follow step-by-step instructions

3. FLEXIBLE
   └── Pantry changed? Tap 'Change Ingredients' → regenerate instantly
